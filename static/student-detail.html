My Story

59 year old Sougaijam Janani Devi lives in the village of Ngaikhong Khulen in Bishnupur, Manipur. She lives with her husband and two sons. She has been running a petty shop for the last 20 years and sells almost all items of daily need right from snacks to food items to groceries. People of her village never have to go far to buy the items of their requirement because of her shop. Janani Devi now wants to expand her shop so that she can earn more. She has applied for a Rang De loan of Rs. 8000 in order to increase the stocks of goods in her shop.
